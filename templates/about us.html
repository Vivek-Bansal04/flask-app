We would like to introduce ourselves as the 'SPEECHLESS SOUL' which is an coming Non Government and Non Profit Organisation dedicated towards the care and well being of stray and abandoned animals without under any care.
Click to read more
You can sum up a stray or an abandoned animal in a heartbeat. They do not expect much effort other than what basic human etiquette as well as sentiment demands. Things like care, affection, loyalty, responsibility, nurture, friendship and most of all, consideration. If each one of us can imbibe even the smallest amount of considerate effort towards these animals on the street, we will not only be better at our attempt at showing basic human instinct, but also, and much more importantly, depict true protectiveness for those that cannot look out for themselves. They don't ask anything in return. How can they? They cannot speak. But they will definitely adore you, for that one small act of kindness, goes a long way towards determining the kind of person that you are. So, here is your opportunity to join us in our efforts at making this world a much better place for strays and abandoned animals than it is currently. If you have spotted or know of any stray or abandoned animals in need of help in your vicinity then please contact us and we will do the needful. This is our stand to help those that deserve it. Will you stand with us?.
